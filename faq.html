  • FAQ
  • What are the qualifications required to become a nurse?

    We require registered practicing nurses who have completed atleast 3-4 years of schooling and are certified by their respective country's nursing bodies

  • How long does the process take?

    The speed of the process depends on how quickly you can provide the necessary documents and make your payments. However, the process typically takes between 6-8 month

  • What is the total cost of the registration process?

    The total cost will vary depending on the applicant. We recommend downloading our handbook and reviewing it to get a better understanding of the expected costs.

  • Can I register on behalf of someone else?

    Yes, as long as you can provide the required details in the registration form, you are permitted to register on behalf of someone else. However, the individual involved will need to take over the process at a certain point.

  • Can I apply directly to the agency?

    Yes, to an appointed Agent, who is accredited and will be verified by LMN in Nigeria. You can only apply through an appointed Agent.

  • Can I apply for other countries other than Canada?

    At present, we are only focused on nursing registration in Canada. However, if you have specific requirements, we may be able to assist you. Please contact us for further information.
