About LMNurse

LMNurse hires international nurses and personal support workers (PSW) for employment opportunities within our organization. We are committed to providing career opportunities across Canada, offering hundreds of career positions for nurses and PSWs. We support you every step of the way, providing a home away from home and investing in your growth to achieve your career goals. We focus on delivering quality, professional healthcare services to our home care clients. Our nursing team is registered with the College of Nurses of Ontario and the Nursing Association of Ontario, and our PSWs are qualified home care professionals dedicated to quality home care services for all our clients, regardless of race and gender. We are founded on quality, compassion, professionalism, and the promotion of health and wellness. LMNurse is dedicated to your success and well-being.
